**Here are relevant human blogs, Medium articles, and Christian-labeled resources discussing the "Mandela Effect" (or "Mandella Effect") in connection with Bible changes.** These focus on exploration, personal experiences, testimonies, or affirmations of supernatural/observed changes (often tied to KJV or other versions), without primarily aiming to debunk or disprove the phenomenon.

### Dedicated Christian/Scripture-Focused Sites and Blogs:
- **bible-changes.com** (Supernatural Bible Changes | Mandela Effect): A primary resource site by a Christian author documenting extensive claimed supernatural changes to Bibles (including physical copies at home), commentaries, and more. It frames this as satanic assault/end-times deception, with books available on Amazon, testimonies, and lists of examples (e.g., wording shifts, doctrinal implications). Strongly affirmative of the changes as real.
- **thesupernaturalbiblechanges.com**: Similar Christian-oriented site viewing Bible alterations as the "strong delusion," linked to CERN/quantum influences and black magic. Includes change lists and community resources for believers noticing the shifts.

These sites often include personal testimonies and are positioned within Christian contexts warning about deception while affirming the observations.
