# Rare word witness batch 0013 — proposals (entries 481–520)

## bucket → vessel — Isaiah 40:15
- verdict: REVISE
- rationale: "vessel" is genuinely different, extremely well attested in KJV (1197 occurrences), and preserves the sense of a drop from a container.
- NEW: Behold, the nations are as a drop of a vessel, and are counted as the small dust of the balance: behold, he taketh up the isles as a very little thing.
