# Rare word witness batch 0025 — proposals (entries 961–1000)

## dale → valley — Genesis 14:17
- verdict: REVISE
- rationale: Matches YLT and Darby exactly ("the king's valley"); "valley" is very common in KJV.db and genuinely different from "dale."
- NEW: And the king of Sodom went out to meet him after his return from the slaughter of Chedorlaomer, and of the kings that were with him, at the valley of Shaveh, which is the king's valley.

## dale → valley — II Samuel 18:18

## damnable → pernicious — II Peter 2:1
- verdict: REVISE
- rationale: CUR "perdition heresies" is ungrammatical — "perdition" is a noun and cannot modify "heresies" as an adjective. ACV renders this verse "pernicious denominations"; "pernicious" is attested in KJV.db (7 occurrences) and is a genuinely different adjective (a "damnable" heresy is one that leads to ruin — "pernicious" preserves that sense grammatically).
- NEW: But there were false prophets also among the people, even as there shall be false teachers among you, who privily shall bring in pernicious heresies, even denying the Lord that bought them, and bring upon themselves swift destruction.
