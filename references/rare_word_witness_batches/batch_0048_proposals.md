## gutters → channels — Genesis 30:38
- verdict: REVISE
- rationale: CUR "galleries" does not fit a watering-trough context. "gutters" itself is attested only 3x in KJV.db (all in this Jacob narrative), confirming its rarity; "channels" is genuinely different, attested 4x in KJV.db, and names the same kind of water conduit.
- NEW: And he set the rods which he had pilled before the flocks in the channels in the watering troughs when the flocks came to drink, that they should conceive when they came to drink.
