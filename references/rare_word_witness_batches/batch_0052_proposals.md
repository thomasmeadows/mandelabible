## husks → that which the swine did eat — Luke 15:16
- verdict: REVISE
- rationale: "chaff" (CUR) names threshing waste, not food swine ate — a wrong referent. Rather than substitute one wrong noun for another, the clause is recast on BBE's sense ("the pigs' food") using the wholly common KJV construction "that which the swine did eat" (103 hits for "did eat"; "that which the" 25 hits), which needs no specific noun for the husks/pods at all.
- NEW: And he would fain have filled his belly with that which the swine did eat: and no man gave unto him.
