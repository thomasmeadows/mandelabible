# Rare word witness batch 0057 — proposals

## lately → newly — Acts 18:2
- verdict: REVISE
- rationale: "newly come from Italy" is authentic EModE usage (= "recently arrived"), a genuinely different word from "lately," and though the exact form is rare in KJV.db (2 hits) it is not a modern coinage; no period witness offers a better-attested alternative to "lately" itself.
- NEW: And found a certain Jew named Aquila, born in Pontus, newly come from Italy, with his wife Priscilla; (because that Claudius had commanded all Jews to depart from Rome:) and came unto them.
