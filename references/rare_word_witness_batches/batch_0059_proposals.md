## lock → locks — Ezekiel 8:3
- verdict: REVISE
- rationale: CUR's "a locks" is ungrammatical (singular article with plural noun) and merely re-inflects the flagged word. "hair" (81 hits) matches BBE and BSB directly ("took me by the hair of my head") and restores grammar.
- NEW: And he put forth the form of an hand, and took me by the hair of mine head; and the spirit lifted me up between the earth and the heaven, and brought me in the visions of God to Jerusalem, to the door of the inner gate that looketh toward the north; where was the seat of the image of jealousy, which provoketh to jealousy.
