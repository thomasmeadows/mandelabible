## mart → merchant — Isaiah 23:3
- verdict: REVISE
- rationale: "she is a merchant of nations" shifts the sense from Tyre being a trading-hub/marketplace to being a single trader. "market" (17 hits) matches RWebster's own rendering of this verse exactly and preserves the sense of a hub of commerce.
- NEW: And by great waters the seed of Sihor, the harvest of the river, is her revenue; and she is a market of nations.

- NEW: And when the blood of thy witness Stephen was shed, I also was standing by, and consenting unto his death, and kept the raiment of them that slew him.
