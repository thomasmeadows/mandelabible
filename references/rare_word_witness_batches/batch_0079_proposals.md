# Rare word witness batch 0079 — proposals

## reaper → proposed(mower) — Amos 9:13
- verdict: REVISE
- rationale: CUR "reap" is the wrong part of speech ("overtake the reap" is ungrammatical, a noun is required). Geneva reads "the plowman shall touche the mower." "Mower" (1 hit, nonzero) is genuinely different from "reaper" and grammatically sound.
- NEW: Behold, the days come, saith the Lord, that the plowman shall overtake the mower, and the treader of grapes him that soweth seed; and the mountains shall drop sweet wine, and all the hills shall melt.
