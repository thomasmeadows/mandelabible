- verdict: REVISE
- rationale: CUR "thou refuse" drops the -edst ending (rule 3 violation) and breaks the required past-tense second-person form. RWebster reads "thou didst refuse to be ashamed," restoring correct grammar with a genuinely different construction.
- NEW: Therefore the showers have been withholden, and there hath been no latter rain; and thou hadst a whore's forehead, thou didst refuse to be ashamed.
