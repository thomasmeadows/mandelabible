# Rare word witness batch 0083 — proposals

## richer → exceed — Daniel 11:2
- verdict: REVISE
- rationale: CUR "far rich than they all" drops the comparative, breaking grammar. "exceed" is attested in the KJV corpus (3 hits) and Darby's "enrich himself...more than all" confirms the comparative sense; rephrasing as "exceed them all in riches" preserves the meaning without re-inflecting "rich".
- NEW: And now will I shew thee the truth. Behold, there shall stand up yet three kings in Persia; and the fourth shall exceed them all in riches: and by his strength through his riches he shall stir up all against the realm of Grecia.
