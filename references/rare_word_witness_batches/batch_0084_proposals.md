# Rare word witness batch 0084 — proposals

## rude → unlearned — II Corinthians 11:6
- verdict: REVISE
- rationale: CUR "ignorant in speech, yet not in knowledge" reads as self-contradictory. Wycliffe/YLT render this exact verse "unlearned in word"; "unlearned" is attested in the corpus (6 hits) and preserves Paul's meaning — untrained as an orator, but not lacking in knowledge — without inverting it.
- NEW: But though I be unlearned in speech, yet not in knowledge; but we have been throughly made manifest among you in all things.
