## sooner → ariseth — James 1:11
- verdict: REVISE
- rationale: CUR's "no rise risen" is nonsensical (word duplicated against "risen"). ASV/DRC render this clause without the "no sooner...but" idiom at all — "the sun ariseth/rose with...heat" — a clause rewrite that removes the rare comparative entirely, per witness precedent.
- NEW: For the sun ariseth with a burning heat, but it withereth the grass, and the flower thereof falleth, and the grace of the fashion of it perisheth: so also shall the rich man fade away in his ways.
