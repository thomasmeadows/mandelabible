# Batch 0100 proposals

## task → portion — Exodus 5:14
- verdict: KEEP
- rationale: CUR's "portion" matches YLT exactly ("your portion in making brick") and is common in the KJV corpus.
- NEW: And the officers of the children of Israel, which Pharaoh's taskmasters had set over them, were beaten, and demanded, Wherefore have ye not fulfilled your portion in making brick both yesterday and to day, as heretofore?

## task → work — Exodus 5:19
- verdict: REVISE
- rationale: CUR's "word" is nonsensical. Darby renders "the daily work"; "work" is extremely common in the KJV corpus and preserves the sense of the daily brick-quota.
- NEW: And the officers of the children of Israel did see that they were in evil case, after it was said, Ye shall not minish ought from your bricks of your daily work.
