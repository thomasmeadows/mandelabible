# Batch 0109 proposals

## wanderers → pourers — Jeremiah 48:12
- verdict: REVISE
- rationale: CUR's "captives, that shall cause him to wander" is nonsensical (captives do not cause others to wander). Darby renders "pourers that shall pour him off," matching the wine-vessel metaphor of the verse (bottles emptied and broken); ASV likewise "them that pour off."
- NEW: Therefore, behold, the days come, saith the Lord, that I will send unto him pourers, that shall cause him to wander, and shall empty his vessels, and break their bottles.

## wanderers → wander — Hosea 9:17
- verdict: REVISE
- rationale: "wanderers" is itself the flagged word and must go. Geneva1599 attests the verb form "they shall wander among the nations" verbatim — a legitimate rephrase (noun clause to verb clause) with strong period support.
- NEW: My God will cast them away, because they did not hearken unto him: and they shall wander among the nations.
