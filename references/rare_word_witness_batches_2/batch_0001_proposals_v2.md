# Round-2 rare-word proposals — batch 0001 (groups 1–40), REVISED RULES

Per the Premise Revision, no corpus — including KJV.db / KJV_restored itself — is treated as self-certifying. All "attestation" citations below are to witness translations (Wycliffe, Tyndale, Geneva1599, DRC, Webster, RWebster, YLT, Darby, ASV, UKJV, ACV, BBE, BSB) and to the `word_counts` table for KJV_restored, used only to confirm a replacement candidate is not itself a new rare word. Verdicts are advisory.
