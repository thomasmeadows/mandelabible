# Batch 0002 proposals — groups 41–80

Method note: for every group I first queried the base text `bible_databases/formats/sqlite/KJV.db` (the project's authoritative, pre-restoration source) for the exact verse. In the great majority of entries below, KJV.db's own reading at that verse differs from the CUR (restored) reading — that mismatch is treated as the strongest available replacement evidence, stronger than raw corpus frequency in `db/mandela.db`, because it shows what word actually stood in the authoritative base text before the rare-word swap was introduced. Where KJV.db's reading is IDENTICAL to CUR, that is treated as evidence the CUR word is itself the genuine, simply-rare 1611 reading (WHITELIST), not a corruption.
