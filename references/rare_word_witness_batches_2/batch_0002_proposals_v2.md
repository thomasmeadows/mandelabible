## besiegers → spoilers — Jeremiah 4:16
- verdict: REPLACE
- suggestion: "spoilers" (count 8) — a common KJV word for raiders/plunderers that compass a city, matching the sense of armed men come to besiege.
- NEW: Make ye mention to the nations; behold, publish against Jerusalem, that spoilers come from a far country, and give out their voice against the cities of Judah.
- rationale: "besiegers" is a corpus hapax; "spoilers" is well attested (8) and names the same referent (hostile raiders approaching a city) without inventing new vocabulary. Witnesses split between "watchers" and "besiegers/attackers" — "spoilers" sits comfortably in that semantic field.
